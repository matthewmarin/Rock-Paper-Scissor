Rock, Paper, Scissors is a classic game played between two players. Each player simultaneously chooses one of three hand gestures: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
The game follows simple rules: Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock. Rounds are played, and the winner of each round earns a point.
The game is typically played in a best-of-odd-number series, with the first player to win the majority of rounds declared the overall winner.
While some players may employ strategies, luck remains a significant factor in determining the outcome. Fair play and honesty are crucial for an enjoyable experience.
